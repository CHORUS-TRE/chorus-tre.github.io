# CHORUS Community

CHORUS is developed and operated as a community-driven platform, and it is deployed by different institutions and research communities, each running its own environment with locally defined governance, datasets, tools, and support processes. While the core CHORUS platform follows the same security, privacy, and Trusted Research Environment principles everywhere, each deployment reflects the needs, regulations, and scientific focus of its local community and maintains its own dedicated documentation.
